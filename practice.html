Team Meeting Update
Intermediate 3 min

Give a concise project status update to your team, covering progress, blockers, and next steps in under 3 minutes.

Change Scenario
Session Timer
0:00

Structure Guide

1

Opening Hook (30 sec)

Start with the single most important point. State the current project status in one clear sentence before providing any background.

2

Progress Summary (1 min)

What has been completed since the last meeting? Be specific with milestones. Use numbers where possible — percentages, dates, deliverables.

3

Blockers and Risks (45 sec)

Name any obstacles honestly. For each blocker, briefly suggest what you need from the team or leadership to resolve it.

4

Next Steps (45 sec)

List 2-3 concrete actions planned for the coming days. Assign ownership if speaking on behalf of a team.

General Tips
Speaking Fundamentals
Pause intentionally. Silence is not weakness — it signals confidence and gives the audience time to absorb what you said.
Use concrete nouns and active verbs. Vague language dilutes impact. "We shipped the dashboard" beats "there has been progress."
End with a clear, single ask or takeaway. Never let your audience wonder what you want them to do next.
